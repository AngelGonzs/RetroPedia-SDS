About this Wiki

Welcome to RetroPedia, the ultimate source for information on classic and retro video games! Our mission is to preserve the rich history of video games and make it accessible to everyone.

Whether you're a seasoned gamer or new to the scene, you'll find something to love on RetroPedia. Our contributors are passionate about gaming and are committed to providing accurate and engaging content on everything from the earliest arcade games to some later bangers.
